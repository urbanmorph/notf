    About

    What is NOTF?

    Neighborhoods of the Future (NOTF) is built on a simple principle: neighborhoods already hold the knowledge, relationships, and initiatives needed for transformation.

    • Connects local efforts, making them visible, adaptable, and actionable.
    • bringing together community groups, local organizations, thematic experts, and institutions.
    • Creates a shared canvas for collaboration — where actors contribute what they can and access what they need.

    NOTF is not a program or a group but an infrastructure for facilitation and alignment, enabling neighborhoods to be self-directed, responsive, and resilient.
